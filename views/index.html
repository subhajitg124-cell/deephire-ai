About DeepHire

DeepHire is an AI-powered hiring assistant that uses Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning to analyze resumes and intelligently match candidates with suitable job roles.

The platform helps reduce manual screening time and enables smarter hiring decisions by automatically predicting the most relevant job category for each resume.
